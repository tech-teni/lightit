Application Process

In order to be enrolled at Vokeseel School, all prospective students/pupils must meet and comply with the criteria for admission. Prospective families are encouraged to schedule a school visit by contacting the Admission Office.
Most students/pupils are admitted at the beginning of the school year.
There will be an invitation to write an examination. The first round of admission decision will be e-mailed to candidate's parents or guardians within a week after the examination.
After this, applications will be considered based on candidate's performance and available space.
