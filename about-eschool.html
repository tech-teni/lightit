What is E-School (Electronic School Management System)

e-School is a state-of-the-art online school management and administration system. It has comprehensive set of modules for all school functions and online access for management, teachers, parents, students, Accountants etc.

e-School is a unique solution catering for the needs of schools. In addition to all the basic modules, E-School has mobile applications Android & IOS to provide on-the-go communication among school admin, teachers, parents, students, Accountants etc.

The Application adds quality to academic institutions through efficient monitoring of the day to day progress and performance of the students. What made us the best is our features that allows parents to stay updated with their kidsâ€™ performance levels.

How to access E-School

The school Lightit School has registered on eschool, download the application on your phone to access the application.
You can also access the application on your desktop or laptop devices via the web.

How to use E-School

  1. Download the Application
  2. Click on Category: "Parent"
  3. Login
    Username: Child's Admission Number
    Password: Given to you by the school administrator
  4. Communication Diary
    New Message: To compose message to the teacher you wish to communicate with.
    Inbox: To Check messages sent to you.
    Sent: To check messages sent by you.
  5. Students Result
    Click on check result to view result of your child

Benefits of E-School
  • Cloud based. No Server or IT infrastructure required to manage the solution
  • Secured platform Https with SSL Certificate
  • Online & Realtime Communication between the teachers, parents and the school administration
  • Provides access to view and download student result
  • Photobook for students per class
  • Remote online tracking of student activities
  • Reliable data storage
  • Free training for teachers, parents etc.
  • Available in google play store and app world for andriod and IOS devices. Also available in web access via your laptops and desktops.
